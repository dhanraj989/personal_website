Image Denoising of Handwritten Devanagari Script Images (Bachelor Thesis Project I)

Duration: Aug 2023 - Nov 2023

Institution: Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur

Supervisor: Professor BG Menon, Industrial and Systems Engineering Department, IIT Kharagpur

Objective: The primary aim of this research is to develop and evaluate image-denoising algorithms that can effectively reduce noise in handwritten Devanagari script images.

  • Created a denoising dataset utilizing IIIT-HW-Dev Dataset for ground truth images and generated corresponding noise images by adding Gaussian, Salt and Pepper, Poisson, and Speckle noises
  • Trained PRIDNet, RIDNet, Boosted Residual Dense UNet, and Pix2Pix GAN models on a smaller denoising dataset made by combining two datasets, SIDD and RENOIR
  • Took 2 architectures that performed well on the smaller dataset; fine-tuned them on the created denoising dataset, also trained both architectures from scratch on the denoising dataset created
  • Obtained an impressive Peak Signal to Noise Ratio (PSNR) of about 38dB for the best architecture on the test dataset
